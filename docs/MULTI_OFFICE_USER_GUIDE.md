## Understanding Your Office Assignment

### What is Office Assignment?

When your account was created, you were assigned to a specific office location (e.g., "Prem Darvaja Office" or "Aslali Office"). This assignment determines:

- Which data you can see
- Where your transactions are stored
- Which reports you can access

### Your Office Assignment

Your assigned office is displayed in the header of every screen:

```
[Your Name | Your Role | Your Office]
```

For example:
```
John Doe | Majur | Prem Darvaja Office
```

### Important Notes

- You can only see data from your assigned office
- You cannot switch to other offices (only admins can do this)
- All your transactions are automatically tagged with your office
- You cannot access data from other offices

## Office Indicator

### Where to Find It

The office indicator is always visible in the **header** at the top of every screen. It shows:

- Your name
- Your role (Admin, Majur, Normal User)
- Your assigned office

### What It Means

The office name in the header tells you:
- Which office's data you're currently viewing
- Where your new transactions will be saved
- Which location's reports you're accessing

### Visual Appearance

For regular users, the office name is displayed as **static text** (not clickable). Only admins have a dropdown to switch offices.

## Creating Transactions

### Automatic Office Tagging

When you create any transaction, it is **automatically tagged** with your assigned office. You don't need to do anything special.

**Supported Transactions:**
- Agency Payments
- Agency Majuri
- Driver Transactions
- Truck Fuel Entries
- General Entries
- Uppad/Jama Entries
- Cash Records

### Verification

Before saving important transactions:
1. Check the office indicator in the header
2. Verify it shows your correct office
3. Proceed with creating the transaction

### Example Workflow

1. Navigate to **Add General Entry**
2. Notice the header shows: `[Your Name | Your Role | Prem Darvaja Office]`
3. Fill in the entry details
4. Tap **Save**
5. The entry is automatically saved to "Prem Darvaja Office"

## Viewing Reports

### Daily Reports

When you view daily reports:
- You see only transactions from your assigned office
- Totals are calculated for your office only
- Other offices' data is not visible

### Monthly Statements

When you view monthly statements:
- Data is filtered by your assigned office
- Historical data from your office is included
- Comparisons are within your office only

### PDF Reports

When you generate PDF reports:
- The report header includes your office name
- All transactions are from your office
- Totals reflect your office's data only

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I switch to another office?

**No.** Only admin users can switch between offices. Regular users are restricted to their assigned office.

### Can I see data from other offices?

**No.** For data security and clarity, you can only see data from your assigned office.

### What if I work at multiple offices?

Contact your admin to discuss your situation. Currently, users can only be assigned to one office at a time.

### What happens to my old data if my office changes?

If an admin changes your office assignment:
- Your historical transactions remain in the original office
- You will only see data from your new office going forward
- Your old data is not lost, but you won't see it anymore

### How do I know which office I'm assigned to?

Look at the header on any screen. Your office name is displayed next to your name and role.

### Can I create transactions for another office?

**No.** All your transactions are automatically tagged with your assigned office. You cannot create transactions for other offices.

### What if I don't see any data?

Possible reasons:
- Your office might not have any transactions yet
- You might not have the proper screen access permissions
- There might be a connectivity issue

**Solution**: Contact your admin or supervisor for assistance.

### What if the office indicator shows the wrong office?

This should not happen. If you see the wrong office name:
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Check with your admin about your office assignment
3. Contact support if the issue persists

### Can I request to be moved to another office?

Yes, contact your admin. They can change your office assignment in the User Access Management screen.

## Best Practices

### Before Creating Transactions

1. **Verify Office**: Always check the office indicator in the header
2. **Confirm Details**: Ensure you're entering data for the correct date and party
3. **Double-Check**: Review all details before saving

### When Viewing Reports

1. **Check Office**: Verify the office indicator shows your office
2. **Date Range**: Ensure you're viewing the correct date range
3. **Totals**: Review totals to ensure they match your expectations

### If You Have Questions

1. **Check Header**: Verify your office assignment in the header
2. **Ask Admin**: Contact your admin for office-related questions
3. **Review Guide**: Refer back to this guide for clarification
